About Us

In a third world country like Bangladesh,agriculture is one ofthe largest sector. About 50 %of the population are employed in this sector and about 70 %people overall depend on agriculture for their livelihood.The average share of agriculture as a proportion of GDPis 29.65%as of 2019.(Imdad, 2021)According to the World Bank, the total arable land in Bangladesh is 61.2 percent of the total land area. (Nations Encyclopedia)Despite these statistics,it is a matter of sorrow that the Backbone of this sector, the farmers are not getting their fair share of the part as different types of middlemenare engaging in their trade. For this reason,farmersare being underpaid and losing their interest in farming, which may lead to an economical breakdown.Our solution to this problem is Krishok Bangla which is an Agricultural Management System. It is a digital B2B market solution which will help farmers and dealers to connect with each other and allow the dealers to buy crops and vegetables from farmers all over Bangladesh. This agricultural management system will work as a bridge between farmers and dealers and would decrease many hassles of traditional crop buying methods and would increase profitability and efficiency for both farmers and the dealers.
